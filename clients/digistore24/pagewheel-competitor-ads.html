How to use this

  1. Click Google Ads Transparency on each competitor to see every active ad they are running today in the US.
  2. Click Meta Ads Library to see their Facebook + Instagram ads, including video creative.
  3. Screenshot the best-performing-looking ones — Google sorts the Transparency feed by ad spend, so the top results are typically their highest-priority campaigns.
  4. Note headline and offer patterns across competitors — repeated themes tell you what the category responds to (e.g., "no tech skills needed", "X minutes to launch", AI-assisted templates).
