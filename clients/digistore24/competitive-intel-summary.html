Background & Purpose

This analysis was conducted on behalf of Digistore24 to provide context for the competitive landscape in Paid Search advertising. After a preliminary review of the Digistore24 Google Ads account, it was determined that there was not enough prior campaign history to conduct a proper performance audit.

Instead, this comprehensive competitive intelligence suite was compiled to provide a roadmap and set of tools for building a full-faceted Google Search Ads strategy from the ground up.

The analysis covers six key competitors in the affiliate network and digital marketplace space: ClickBank, Impact, Awin, MaxWeb, Realize, and SamCart. Data was sourced from SimilarWeb, Google Keyword Planner, and Google Ads Transparency Center.

What This Tool Suite Provides

  • Competitor ad creative examples from Google Ads Transparency
  • Landing page analysis with design patterns and conversion strategies
