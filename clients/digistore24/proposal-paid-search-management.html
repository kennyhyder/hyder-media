Section 01

Executive Summary

This proposal outlines a 10-month paid search management engagement designed to take Digistore24 from zero paid search presence to a fully scaled, profitable acquisition channel spending $350–450K/month.

The engagement is structured in three phases: a focused Build & Test phase (months 1–3) to establish the account, validate unit economics, and achieve profitability; a Scale phase (months 4–7) to systematically increase spend while maintaining performance; and a Optimize at Scale phase (months 8–10) to reach target velocity and maximize efficiency.

Fees are structured as a tiered percentage of ad spend, ensuring alignment at every stage: lower total cost during the testing phase, and a decreasing effective rate as the account scales. A performance incentive creates additional upside for both parties when results exceed targets.

How Marginal Tiers Work

At $275,000 in monthly spend, the fee calculation is: ($100,000 × 8%) + ($150,000 × 6%) + ($25,000 × 4%) = $8,000 + $9,000 + $1,000 = $18,000 (6.5% effective rate). The effective rate naturally decreases as spend scales.

Section 06

Scaling Framework

Spend increases are gated by performance to ensure profitability is established before scaling. This protects both parties: Digistore24's capital isn't deployed into an underperforming channel, and the engagement maintains the metrics needed for long-term success.

Performance Gates

Monthly Spend Level Requirement to Scale Beyond
$25K – $100K No gate — testing and learning phase
Above $100K Trailing 30-day ROAS ≥ 1.0X (inclusive of fees)
Above $250K Trailing 30-day ROAS ≥ 1.3X (inclusive of fees)
Spend Adjustments

If performance dips below the gate threshold for a given spend level, spend will be held or reduced until performance recovers. The spend ramp outlined in Section 03 is a target trajectory, not a commitment — actual spend will be adjusted based on real-time performance data.

Projected Ramp

Months 1–3: Build & Test ($25K – $108K/mo)
Account build-out, campaign launches, conversion tracking validation, initial keyword and audience testing. Goal: establish baseline metrics and prove break-even viability by end of month 3.
Months 4–7: Scale ($150K – $275K/mo)
Systematic spend increases across proven campaigns. Keyword expansion, new ad formats (Performance Max, RSAs), audience layering. Weekly pacing reviews to stay on trajectory.
Months 8–10: Optimize at Scale ($317K – $400K/mo)
Efficiency optimization at target velocity. Portfolio bid strategies, diminishing returns analysis, channel expansion evaluation (Microsoft Ads, YouTube). Quarterly business review to plan next phase.

Upon completion of the initial term, the engagement auto-renews on a quarterly basis under identical terms (same tiered fee structure, same performance incentive) unless either party provides 60 days' written notice of non-renewal.

  • Setup fee: Invoiced upon signing. Due prior to commencement of work.
  • Monthly management fee: Invoiced on the 1st of each month based on the prior month's actual ad spend. Net 15 terms.
  • Performance bonus: Invoiced quarterly (if applicable), within 15 days of quarter close. Net 15 terms.

Account Ownership

All Google Ads accounts, campaign assets, conversion data, keyword lists, and ad copy created during the engagement remain the property of Digistore24. Hyder Media will have managed access to the account for the duration of the engagement.

Early Termination

Either party may terminate the engagement with 30 days' written notice after the initial 3-month Build & Test phase. During months 1–3, the engagement may only be terminated for cause. All fees for services rendered through the termination date remain due.

Reporting & Transparency

Digistore24 retains full owner access to all advertising accounts at all times. All spend, performance metrics, and optimization actions are visible in real time. No data is proprietary to Hyder Media.

Confidentiality

Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of proprietary business information, including but not limited to: conversion rates, LTV data, customer acquisition costs, campaign strategies, and keyword intelligence shared during this engagement.

Section 08

Why This Structure

Aligned Incentives at Every Stage

During testing: The $5,000 floor ensures meaningful management at low spend without disproportionate cost. You pay for hands-on-keyboard work, not a percentage.

While scaling: Tiered percentages mean the effective rate decreases as spend grows. At $400K/month, the blended rate is under 6% — well below the industry standard of 8–12% for accounts of this size.

At performance: The bonus only triggers when ROAS exceeds 1.5X inclusive of all fees. When it activates, Digistore24 keeps 90% of the excess profit. We only win more when you win significantly more.

On renewal: Same terms continue. No renegotiation, no rate increase. The same tiers apply whether it's month 10 or month 30.
