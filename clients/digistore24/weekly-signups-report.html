Methodology. Vendor and affiliate signup attribution requires two data sources because Google Ads couldn't distinguish them until May 1.

April 10 – April 22 (approximate): GA4 BigQuery live export only goes back to ~April 23. For the earlier window, values are distributed uniformly from the 28-day historical backfill aggregate (Mar 30 – Apr 26). These weeks are tagged approx in the table. The split between vendor and affiliate is reliable; per-day volume within the week is a uniform-distribution estimate, not a per-day actual.

April 23 – April 30: GA4 BigQuery live export, split by the acct_type event parameter set on signup_success. Filtered to sessions where last-click Google Ads customer_id = 2466246400 (Digistore24's account).

May 1 – today: Google Ads conversion actions Vendor Sign-up + Affiliate Sign-up via the v23 API. The legacy combined conversion action ran in parallel from April 30 through May 2 and was disabled May 3, so the May 1 cutover prevents double-counting.
