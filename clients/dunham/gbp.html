Quick Setup — GBP Auto-Extract

Extract data directly from your Google Business Profile Manager — no manual copy/paste needed.

1
Drag this to your bookmarks bar:
GBP Extract Drag it, don't click it!
2
Go to business.google.com and open a location's "Edit profile"
3
Click the "GBP Extract" bookmark. A toolbar appears with options:
  • Send to Hyder Media — extracts text data and saves it here automatically
  • Copy All Text — copies the extracted text to your clipboard
  • Fix Scroll for Screenshot — expands the panel so you can take a full screenshot
4
Repeat for each location. Click through each tab (About, Contact, Hours, etc.) before extracting to capture more data.
