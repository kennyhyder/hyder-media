Quick Setup — GBP Auto-Extract

Extract data directly from your Google Business Profile Manager — no manual copy/paste needed.

1
Drag this to your bookmarks bar:
GBP Extract Drag it, don't click it!
2
Go to business.google.com and click the pencil icon to edit a location
3
Click the "GBP Extract" bookmark. A toolbar appears with options:
  • Send to Hyder Media — extracts text data and saves it here automatically
  • Copy All Text — copies the panel text to your clipboard
  • Fix Scroll — expands the panel so you can take a full screenshot
4
Repeat for each location. Click through each tab (About, Contact, Hours, etc.) before extracting to capture more data.
Alternative: Console paste method

If the bookmarklet doesn't work, you can paste the script directly into the browser console:

  1. On business.google.com, press F12 (or Cmd+Option+J on Mac) to open DevTools
  2. Click the Console tab
  3. Click the button below to copy the script, then paste it in the console and press Enter
