X. CONCLUSION

Auto Glass 2020 LLC represents a salvageable business with proven historical success. The company grew from startup to Arizona's 2nd largest auto glass company in just 4 years, completing over 54,000 jobs with consistent 51% gross margins. This demonstrates the fundamental viability of the business model and strong market demand.

The recent decline was caused by the owner's personal struggles (relapse and divorce), not by market conditions or business fundamentals. With proper oversight through the Subchapter V Trustee's expanded powers, realistic projections based on actual performance, and owner accountability, the business can stabilize and begin recovery.

Under Chapter 11, unsecured creditors will receive approximately 16% recovery - infinitely better than the 0% they would receive in Chapter 7 liquidation. The business has existing insurance company relationships, trained staff (though reduced), and operational infrastructure that would be lost in liquidation.

The January 2026 revised plan, while showing minimal cash flow margins, is based on actual performance data and represents a realistic (if challenging) path forward. The key to success is immediate cash infusion, professional management, and continued Trustee oversight.
