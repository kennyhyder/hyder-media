The Problem

Our Solution

SolarScore aggregates the fragmented installer network with quality scores, standardized quoting, and performance tracking—becoming the 'network of record' that lenders, utilities, and homeowners trust.

The Perfect Storm

ITC Cliff Reshapes Market

Dec 2025 ITC expiration for homeowners pushes market to TPO models. Third-party owners (lenders, utilities) need installer quality data to protect their long-term assets.

Storage Attachment Surging

Battery attachment rates hitting 30%+ in key markets (CA, TX, FL). Storage adds complexity—installers need certification, lenders need quality assurance for higher ticket installs.

Lender Consolidation

Solar lending consolidating around Mosaic, Sunlight, GoodLeap. Fewer, larger players with budgets and willingness to pay for installer intelligence.

Grid Reliability Focus

Utilities investing heavily in distributed energy as grid infrastructure. Need installer networks for low-income programs, virtual power plants, demand response.

AI/Data Timing

Machine learning models can now predict installer quality from diverse data sources. First mover to aggregate data wins the network effect.
